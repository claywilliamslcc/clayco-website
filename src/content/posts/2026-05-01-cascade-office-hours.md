Most students don't go to office hours, and most students who do go in a panic the week before a final. Both groups are leaving a lot on the table.

Office hours are the cheapest, highest-leverage academic resource on a campus. A fifteen-minute conversation with a professor or tutor about a concept you almost understand will outperform two hours of staring at the textbook every time. The problem isn't the resource — it's the social activation cost of using it.

Our pitch is simple: we make the activation cost zero. Book a tutor in three clicks. They come to you. Show up with a question, leave with one less.
