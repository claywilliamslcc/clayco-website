We are the Grown Responsibly Equitable Eating Network proudly emerging to serve Lane County through Green Neighbor Garden and Green Neighbor Grocery.

We are committed to providing affordable, locally and sustainably grown, organic produce alongside local artisan goods. In the Green Neighbor Garden, interested community members can grow and harvest fresh produce and attend educational community workshops.

Lane County recipients of SNAP, WIC, free/reduced school lunch, or other income assistance programs are eligible for additional benefits, from G.R.E.E.N., to use right in our store.
