About ClayCo

At ClayCo, we are dedicated to showcasing the innovative businesses created by students. Our platform provides a space for students to exhibit their entrepreneurial ventures and connect with potential collaborators and investors.

We believe in empowering the next generation of leaders and change-makers by giving them the opportunity to present their unique business ideas and projects.

Join us in celebrating the creativity and ambition of student entrepreneurs. Whether you are looking to explore inspiring ventures or share your own, ClayCo is the perfect platform for you.

Sustainable bike repair and refurbishment · Eugene, OR · Spring 2026 · Active

Riverside Bicycle Co. believes the most sustainable bike is one that already exists. We repair, refurbish, and rehome bicycles that would otherwise end up in a landfill — sourced from estate sales, OSU surplus, and donations.

Promo Video: How We Source Frames

Riverside Bicycle Co. · May 4, 2026 · Promo Video

[embedded video — 16:9 placeholder]

We started Riverside on the premise that the most sustainable bike is one that already exists. This video walks through where our frames come from — estate sales, OSU surplus, donations from cyclists upgrading their rides.

The short version: we don't buy new. We buy used, fix what's broken, and pass it on at a price a student can afford. The full pipeline is in the video above, but the gist is that there's a lot of perfectly good steel sitting in garages, and our job is to get it back on the road.

If you've got a frame collecting dust, our donation form is here. We pick up anywhere in Eugene.
